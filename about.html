Hi! I am Riley Owens, a postbaccalaureate astronomer at the University of Cincinnati. I primarily study the early universe by leveraging gravitational lensing, wherein large masses distort space and highly magnify distant background galaxies. Outside of my research, I maintain strong interests in practicing and advocating good pedagogy and mentorship in science. I also have various amateur passions for history, transportation, architecture, and urban design.

I'm always happy to make new collaborators or speak to members of the media about science and astronomy. Feel free to reach out!
